1.
No ultrasound cleaning - RTC clock part U2 is sensitive to vibration.

2.
LCD parts are to be hand soldered. They mount with the LCD face on the FRONT side of the PCB. Back of LCD must have clearance above parts below. Use a jig or 
shim to maintain uniform standoff between LCD and PCB while solderning. 

3.
LCD parts are heat sensitive. Avoid overheating when soldering.

4.
Use the solder reflow profile for RTC clock part U2, included in this directory.
